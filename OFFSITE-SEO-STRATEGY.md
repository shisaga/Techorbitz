### B. HARO (Help A Reporter Out)

**Daily Process:**
```

[2-3 paragraphs of expert insight]
- Specific data/statistics
- Real-world examples
- Actionable advice

I'm happy to provide additional insights or quotes if needed.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title], TechXak
Email: [email]
Phone: [phone]
Website: techxak.com
---
```

**Expected Results:**
- Month 1: 5-10 responses ‚Üí 1-2 mentions
- Month 3: 30-50 responses ‚Üí 5-8 mentions
- Month 6: 100+ responses ‚Üí 15-20 mentions

### C. Broken Link Building

**Tools:**
- Ahrefs (Broken Link Checker)
- Check My Links (Chrome Extension)
- Dead Link Checker

**Process:**

**Step 1: Find Broken Links**
```
Ahrefs ‚Üí Site Explorer ‚Üí Enter competitor URL
‚Üí Outgoing Links ‚Üí Broken Links (404)
```

**Step 2: Create Replacement Content**
- Analyze the broken page (use Wayback Machine)
- Create better, updated content
- Make it comprehensive (2,000+ words)

**Step 3: Outreach**
```
Subject: Broken Link on [Page Title]

Hi [Name],

I was researching [topic] and came across your excellent article: [URL]

[Your URL]

It covers:
- [Updated information]
- [Additional insights]
- [Visual aids/data]

Would you consider updating the link?

Thanks for maintaining such a valuable resource!

Best,
[Your Name]
```
