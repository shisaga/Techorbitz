[Your Name]

## üìä Backlink Quality Metrics

### What Makes a Quality Backlink:

**Domain Authority (DA):**
- Excellent: DA 60+
- Good: DA 40-60
- Acceptable: DA 20-40
- Avoid: DA < 20

**Relevance:**
- Same niche (tech/development): 10/10
- Related niche (business/marketing): 7/10
- General news: 5/10
- Unrelated: 0/10 (avoid)

**Link Placement:**
- Editorial content: Best
- Resource page: Good
- Footer/sidebar: Okay
- Comment: Low value

**Anchor Text:**
- Branded (TechXak): 40%
- Exact match (AI implementation): 20%
- Partial match (AI guide): 20%
- Generic (click here): 10%
- URL (techxak.com): 10%
